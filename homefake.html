Mission Statement:

Perspective was started by high schoolers through Girls Who Code for their final project. As teenagers living in the U.S, we wanted to make resources available to other teenagers about the issues that affect us the most, specifically alcohol and marijuana usage, consent, and the Black Lives Matter movement. When using other resources about these subjects, we noticed that most if not all were written from the perspective of someone that was not a high school student. This did not help us because they did not understand what we as teenagers were going through. Through this website we hope to create a safe environment to discuss race, consent, alcohol and drugs.


Black Lives Matter is a national organization which focuses on the movement of black liberation.
Consent is a mutual agreement between people that are going to engage in intimate activity. Without proper communication, intimacy could be unwanted and uncomfortable.
There are risks associated with underage drinking and marijuana such as brain development, long term memory, and other physical damage that can happen when intoxicated. When using alcohol or marijuana it is best to do so in moderation and know what is too much.
